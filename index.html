What is Joga AI?

Joga AI is your personal soccer coach powered by artificial intelligence. Upload your game clips, and our AI analyzes your performance, gives you actionable feedback, and rates your skills—so you can improve faster, anytime. Built for youth players who want to level up, with insights that used to be reserved for the pros.

Key Features

🎯
Pass Detection
Automatically detects and analyzes your passes for accuracy and timing.
⚡

Our Story

Built by Players, for Players

Joga AI was started by a high school soccer player who wanted every kid to have access to world-class coaching—no matter their background or resources. With a small, passionate team, we’re building the future of player development using AI and sharing every step of our journey publicly.

We believe every player deserves feedback, encouragement, and a chance to improve. That’s why we’re documenting our build on YouTube—so you can follow along, learn, and be part of the movement.
